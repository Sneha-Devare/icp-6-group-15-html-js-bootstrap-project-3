Anywere Door

The Anywhere Door is a teleportation portal that leads to any location the user wishes to visit. It is one of the signature items of Doraemon, a robotic cat from the future, who has a special pocket from which he produces secret gadgets.

Computer Pencil

this pencil is a 'smart' pencil. When one writes something with it, it will write the correct answer no matter what (though blank papers are shown to have no effects).

Copying Toast

Memory Bread in the Hindi and English dub, is a gadget that resembles a typical, single-loaf of plain bread. It allows the user to memorize any contents 'printed' on it.

Big Light

When used, Big Light fires a laser which temporarily increases the mass and space occupied by the target to a certain extent for an unspecified amount of time..

Copying Robot

The robot initially appears as a blank humanoid puppet with a red, nose-like button as its switch, used for startup and shutdown. It can mimic multiple attributes of its target, from appearance and physiology to psychology and sentience.

Air Cannon

The Air Cannon (空気砲) is a gadget that is used generally in the Doraemon anime. Practically, it is 'not' a conventional weapon because it cannot run out nor use bullets. It uses high-pressurized, condensed air, and has high recoil with each shot.

Bird Cap

Bird Caps (バードキャップ) is a set of Doraemon's gadget. To the Sky With Bird Cap Nobita and the Winged Braves In the movie, this cap will make the user have wings, but in the anime this cap can be used without the wings.

Dress Up Camera

The Dress Up Camera is able to override the person's attire with whatever image inserted inside it, seemingly destroying the ammunition in the process.

Time Cloth

The Time Cloth is capable of manipulating time on whatever it covers in a backward or forward direction. The item does not need to be fully enclosed over its target to function and doesn't necessarily affect its attachments or certain other attributes.
